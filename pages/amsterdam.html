Amsterdam

Amsterdam means the "dam of the Amstel". It is the constitutional capital of the Netherlands and since the 17th century an important financial and cultural center. The agricultural production, the nurseries with the famous tulips and the mills that today surround the city, are the typical postcards of the country. Moreover, the economy of Amsterdam and the whole of the Netherlands is consolidated in energy companies, financial services, technology and various commercial activities
